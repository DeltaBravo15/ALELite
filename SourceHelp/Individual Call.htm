Individual Call

This option allows you to make a call to one specific station.

After selecting "Individual Call" from the "Trasnmit" menu, you will be asked to enter the Address of the station. You will be presented with a list of the Other Addresses in your configuration file. Select the Other Address you would like to call and then click on the "Ok" button.

Note: The options you specified when configuring the Other Address (channels, Wait for Reply, etc.) will be used.
