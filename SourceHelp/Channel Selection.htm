Changing Channels

Since you will often be specifying different destination stations and frequencies, you will need to create a list of often-used channels.

Before you begin transmitting or receiving messages, you will need to add at least one channel to the channel list.

A channel is a pair of frequencies (one on which to transmit and one on which to receive) that establish a complete circuit for exchanging messages with another station. Each frequency in the pair will have its own attributes. In most cases, the transmit and receive frequencies will be the same.

Once channels have been added, you can edit or delete the channels.
