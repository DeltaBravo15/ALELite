Changing the Baud Rate

To select a Baud Rate for the radio, click on the down arrow under "Baud Rate" and select the baud rate for your radio.

The current choices for this parameter are,

1200
2400
4800
9600
19200

The radio must be set to use the same baud rate as is selected in this step. Usually, the highest baud rate that is supported by both your radio and your COM port is the best option to specify.
