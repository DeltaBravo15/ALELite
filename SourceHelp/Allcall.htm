Allcall

This option allows you to make a call to several stations at the same time.

After selecting "Allcall" from the "Trasnmit" menu, you will be asked to select the Address(es) of the stations to receive the Allcall. You will be presented with a list of the Other Addresses in your configuration file. Select the Other Address(es) you would like to call and then click on the "Ok" button.

Note: The options you specified when configuring the Other Address (channels, Wait for Reply, etc.) will be used.
